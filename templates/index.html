MISSION BRIEFING

Classified KGB Evasion Tactics

The Objective

Welcome, spy. You are stranded deep in enemy territory, and KGB Agent Putin is on your tail. You must pick the perfect spot to hide in the randomly generated concrete labyrinths. If he finds you before his stamina runs out, you're caught. If his batteries die or he gets completely lost... you survive.

The Technology (and its flaws)

Agent Putin is powered by a Deep Q-Network (DQN). The KGB proudly claims this model has an 82% test accuracy. That sounds intimidating, until you realize that means he has a hilarious 18% margin of error where his digital brain simply short-circuits.

He's great at walking down straight hallways, but if you put a few corners in his way, he might just walk in circles forever. Your job is to exploit that exact weakness.

Rules of Engagement

  • Set the Rounds: Decide how many times you want to taunt him before starting the tournament.
  • Pick your Hideout: Click any reachable cell (empty grid block) to set your hiding spot.
  • Tweak the Parameters: You can dynamically change the grid size and wall density between rounds to make the maze harder for him.
  • Whoever has the most points at the end of the tournament wins.
